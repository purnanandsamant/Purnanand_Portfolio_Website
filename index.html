About Me

I'm a lifelong learner with a unique blend of skills in industrial engineering and data science. My journey began in mechanical engineering, where I developed a strong foundation in physical systems and processes. This led me to specialize in industrial engineering with a focus on electronics packaging, combining hardware and software perspectives.

Throughout my career, I've maintained a commitment to process optimization and efficiency, earning my Lean Six Sigma certification and leading numerous improvement initiatives. However, my fascination with technology and data has continually pushed me to expand my horizons.

The COVID-19 pandemic became a catalyst for my deeper dive into data science. I taught myself Python, delved into SQL, and mastered Power BI, applying these skills to enhance my work in industrial engineering. This fusion of traditional engineering principles with cutting-edge data techniques has become my professional signature.

Today, I'm passionate about leveraging data science to revolutionize industrial processes.
